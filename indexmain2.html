Workplace Rights and Fairness

Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013 (POSH Act)

  • What it is: Provides protection against sexual harassment of women at the workplace and creates a safe and secure working environment.
  • Purpose: Establishes a legal framework for addressing sexual harassment, including the formation of Internal Complaints Committees (ICCs) and setting up a grievance redressal mechanism.
  • Significance: Ensures that workplaces are free from harassment and provides a structured process for handling complaints.

Article 14 – Equality Before the Law

  • What it is: Guarantees equality before the law and equal protection of the laws within India.
  • Purpose: Provides protection against discrimination based on gender, including protection from sexual harassment.
  • Significance: Ensures all individuals are treated equally under the law, supporting the right to a safe and non-discriminatory work environment.

Article 15 – Prohibition of Discrimination

  • What it is: Prohibits discrimination on grounds of religion, race, caste, sex, or place of birth.
  • Purpose: Supports the right to be free from discrimination, including sexual harassment, in any public or private space.
  • Significance: Promotes gender equality and ensures protection from sexual harassment at workplaces.

Article 21 – Right to Life and Personal Liberty

  • What it is: Guarantees the right to life and personal liberty, including the right to live with dignity.
  • Purpose: Includes protection against sexual harassment as part of the right to live with dignity.
  • Significance: Ensures that every individual’s right to a safe and respectful work environment is upheld.
