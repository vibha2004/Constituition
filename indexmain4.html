Health Care Rights

Right to Safety

  • Act: Consumer Protection Act, 2019 (CPA)
  • Description: Protects consumers against goods and services that are hazardous to health or life. Ensures that products and services meet quality and safety standards.

Right to Information

  • Act: Consumer Protection Act, 2019 (CPA)
  • Description: Consumers have the right to be informed about the quality, quantity, potency, purity, standard, and price of goods and services. They should receive all necessary information to make an informed choice.

Right to Choose

  • Act: Consumer Protection Act, 2019 (CPA)
  • Description: Ensures that consumers have the freedom to choose from a range of products and services at competitive prices. It prevents unfair trade practices and monopolies.

Right to be Heard

  • Act: Consumer Protection Act, 2019 (CPA)
  • Description: Consumers have the right to be heard and to seek redressal for grievances. This includes the ability to file complaints and to be heard in the resolution process.

Right to Redressal

  • Act: Consumer Protection Act, 2019 (CPA)
  • Description: Provides consumers with the right to seek compensation for any loss or injury suffered due to defective goods, deficient services, or unfair trade practices. The Act outlines mechanisms for filing complaints and seeking remedies.

Right to Consumer Education

  • Act: Consumer Protection Act, 2019 (CPA)
  • Description: Ensures that consumers are educated about their rights and responsibilities. This includes awareness about consumer rights, remedies, and legal recourse.
